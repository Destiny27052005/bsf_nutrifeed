In alignment with United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 3 — Ensure healthy
lives and promote well-being for all), the Otondo Team by DSHub is developing BSF-Nutrifeed
as a scalable, user-centered solution for sustainable poultry feed production.
To ensure adoption across diverse communities—especially among rural users with varying
levels of digital literacy—the success of BSF-Nutrifeed depends heavily on intuitive,
responsive, and accessible user interfaces.
